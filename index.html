OUR MISSION

Our Mission to work on two major goals one is empowerment of Persons with Disabilities, through educational, Vocational and technical trainings and second is sensitizing society about Persons with Disabilities and their Life style.
Competency Based Training (CBT) and personal development opportunities focusing on better employability with strong market linkages Advocacy and referrals for induction of PWDs in both public and private sector organizations, INCD sends enrolled INCD faculty and students for internships, courses, Advanced DIT and vocational trainings.
Technical and Vocational Education and Training (TVET): Making use of information and Communication technology (ICT) in improving the delivery of TVET, it uses formal, non-formal and informal learning especially beneficial for Persons with Disability.
Entrepreneurial and career counseling, work-readiness, placement services, as well as help in setup of small scale online businesses to PWDs. Capacity building for economic and social integration of persons with disabilities and helps assure independence and a confident blending into the community. It nurtures the hopes & dreams of the overwhelmingly increasing population of disabled youth and strives to see them at work!.
Health advice and medical referrals, promotion of physical and sports activities, social and humanitarian-aid services, independent living skills training, psychological counseling and advisory .
Advocacy and efforts towards inclusiveness and accessibility of this special population in all public buildings, corporate and commercial offices as well as streets, outdoor planning and built-up of to make them “PWD’s friendly” INCD is an equal opportunity organization and engages people independent of gender, social status, ethnicity or race etc.
